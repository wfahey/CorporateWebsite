About Us

Made Media Inc. develops digital data capture and marketing solutions using today’s newest mobile technologies. Founded in 2009 by David Engel and Eric Landy, the company was created with the goal of using technology to enhance face-to-face marketing campaigns. Made Media’s current technologies offer the ability to improve customer interactions and gather more qualified, meaningful data.

Based in Montreal, Quebec, our technologies are used across North America, South America and Europe as global organizations continue to integrate the flagship Made Mobile Sales Platform into their operations. Made Media also offers custom mobile, social and software application development services from a world-class team of developers.

The team at Made Media is driven by innovative ideas and a group of computer geniuses that are ready to take on nearly any development challenge.
